# 19 — Monetization Plan

## 1. Core Rule: Anti-Pay-To-Win (No P2W)
*   Buying items with real money must never provide direct combat modifiers, combat damage multipliers, or instant stat increases.
*   Paying players must not receive exclusive weapons or armor that free-to-play players cannot acquire or craft.
*   *Why*: Browser strategy MMORPGs rely on large player counts to form armies. If free players feel disenfranchised by wealthy players, they leave, causing the social and combat systems to collapse.

---

## 2. Monetization Tiers

### A. Gold Token Sales (Central Market Injection)
*   **The Model**: Players purchase **Gold** from the game platform using credit cards or crypto.
*   **Utility**: Gold is used for systemic actions:
    *   Creating a new Corporation (Gold fee).
    *   Renaming a country or creating custom organization tags.
*   **Forex Trade**: Players can list Gold on the public Currency Forex in exchange for local fiat currency (e.g. USD, EUR, gold coins). Free players buy this Gold using currency they earned by working, while paying players acquire local currency to hire employees.
*   **Benefit**: Premium currency flows into the circular economy rather than creating infinite items from thin air.

---

### B. Premium Citizen Subscription ($4.99 / month)
Provides non-combat quality of life improvements and analytics:
*   **Telemetry Dashboards**: Detailed graphs of historical market prices, work productivity trends, and military hit tracking.
*   **Social Customizations**: Golden username color, animated custom avatars, and exclusive profile layout cards.
*   **Convenience**: Alert notifications sent via email or Discord when the player's home region is under attack.

---

### C. Aesthetic Item Skins
*   Custom weapon models (skins only, no damage difference).
*   Unique country profile frame banners.
*   Corporate office theme designs.
