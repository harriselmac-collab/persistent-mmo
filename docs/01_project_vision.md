# 01 — Project Vision & Strategy

## Executive Summary
This project aims to build a next-generation, persistent browser-based strategy MMORPG. Inspired by web classics like *Torn, eRepublik, e-Sim, and Tribal Wars*, the game models a living virtual society where thousands of players interact in real-time. The game features player-driven economies, national political systems, regional wars, and dynamic alliances, all executing in a persistent online environment.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Player-Driven Everything
The game economy, markets, governments, and border maps are dictated strictly by player decisions. There are no developer-owned stores selling items; every product, weapon, raw resource, and factory is owned, mined, manufactured, and traded by players.

### 2. Geopolitical Authenticity
The virtual world is divided into real-world countries and geographic regions. Players elect local congresses, vote for presidents, run ministries of finance, declare wars, deploy troops to capture regions, and establish tax rates on local companies.

### 3. Accessible yet Infinite Complexity
The game must load in seconds on any web browser (desktop, tablet, or mobile) without requiring high-spec graphics cards or large downloads. The barrier to entry is extremely low, but the strategic depth (combining financial engineering, political diplomacy, coordinate military operations, and spy networks) is theoretically infinite.

### 4. Data-Driven Foundation
Every formula, from military damage calculation to company productivity coefficients and tax allocations, is configurable in the database. This allows Live Operations teams to balance the game dynamically without redeploying code.

## Target Audience
*   **Strategy Enthusiasts**: Players who enjoy complex spreadsheets, market arbitrage, supply-chain logistics, and economic engines.
*   **Social & Political Gamers**: Players driven by social positioning, leadership roles, negotiating defense pacts, and governing nations.
*   **Competitive PvP Players**: Combatants who coordinate massive multi-player operations, tactical territorial defense, and alliance skirmishes.
