## Phase Breakdowns

### Phase 1: Foundation & Identity (Weeks 1-4)
*   **User Hub**: Email registration, authentication states, character creator, status panels (energy, location).
*   **Geographic Map**: Dynamic rendering of the region map. Travel mechanics consuming local ticket items.
*   **Database Configs**: Configurable parameters for leveling limits, base energy recovery intervals, and registration rewards.

### Phase 2: Industrial Systems (Weeks 5-8)
*   **Corporations**: Create companies. List open jobs. Hire workers.
*   **Production Engine**: Daily work triggers product creation, consuming energy. Skill progression determines output scaling.
*   **Marketplace**: Double-entry transaction engine for trading items (Food, Weapons, Travel Tickets) in local currencies.

### Phase 3: War & Territory (Weeks 9-12)
*   **Training Grounds**: Daily strength training routines.
*   **Combat Loop**: Deploying to active battlefields. Hitting target gates to shift influence.
*   **Territory Conquering**: Capturing regions to change national borders, redistributing resource bonuses to the winning country.

### Phase 4: Political Institutions (Weeks 13-16)
*   **Elections**: Automated monthly election cycles.
*   **Law Board**: Congressional voting systems (tax rate changes, printing money, declarations of war, printing gold reserves).
*   **Country Treasuries**: Automated tax withholding from marketplace sales directly into national reserves.

### Phase 5: Financial Systems & Organizations (Weeks 17+)
*   **Foreign Exchange Market (Forex)**: Trading gold reserve indicators for local country fiat currencies.
*   **Player Organizations**: Guild banks, shared factory spaces, corporate stock markets.
