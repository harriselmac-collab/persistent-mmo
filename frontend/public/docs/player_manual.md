# Aegis Kingdoms - Player Manual

Welcome to Aegis Kingdoms, a persistent, sandbox medieval simulation where the economy, politics, and warfare are entirely player-driven.

## 1. Onboarding & Progression
- **Leveling**: Perform gathering actions, craft items, or engage in PvE/PvP battles to earn Experience Points (EXP). Leveling up increases your health and unlocks high-tier recipes.
- **Energy**: Every activity (work shift, gather, combat swing, trade craft) consumes energy. Energy regenerates over time based on your character level.

## 2. Resource Gathering
- Select a region on the map and tap **Gather**. Tools like pickaxes speed up stone/iron extraction, while axes speed up wood cutting.

## 3. Industrial Production & Companies
- Buy a company license or apply to work as an employee.
- **Shifts**: Run shifts to refine raw materials (e.g. smelting iron ore and coal into Steel plates).

## 4. Marketplace & Economy
- Trade commodities on the global marketplace.
- Select your price carefully—every sale incurs a localized regional tax.

## 5. Combat & Conquest
- Equip weapons, helms, chestplates, and boots in your backpack.
- Fight PvE monsters for loot drops or mobilize army units to fight territorial wars.
