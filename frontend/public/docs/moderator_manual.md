# Aegis Kingdoms - Moderator Operations Manual

This guide outlines standard protocols for game moderators and support desk staff.

## 1. Infraction Handling & Sanctions
- **Formal Warnings**: Issue for first-time minor chat spamming, resource blocking, or inappropriate behavior.
- **Chat Mutes**: Temporarily mute characters who persist in abusive or toxic behavior in group/trade channels.
- **Suspensions (Bans)**: Issue temporary (24h-72h) or permanent suspensions for botting, exploit abuse, or duplicated items trading.

## 2. Customer Support Ticketing
- Assign tickets to yourself or escalate critical bugs to developers.
- Use **Internal Notes** for context logs visible only to GM staff before posting player-facing responses.

## 3. Appeal Approvals
- Players can submit appeals via tickets. Review evidence logs in the audit trails before approving appeals or lifting bans.
